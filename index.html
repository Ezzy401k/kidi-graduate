Architect by degree, artist by heart. Five years of architecture school might have been intense, but Kidi made sure it was unforgettable — thanks to a circle of friends wild enough to keep the chaotic energy alive.

Her talent knows no bounds: balancing her creative soul as a painter with the fast-paced hustle of a call center, she's been building — not just blueprints, but a life on her own terms.

Kidi, you've officially graduated. Keep making art, keep making noise, and never lose that incredible spark that makes every room you walk into a little brighter.

Cheers to Kidi — the architect of her own bright future. The drafting table may rest, but the canvas never will.
